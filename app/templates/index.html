My Timeline

Leaving Highschool

I was advised to pursue TL-HAVO from primary school, but I chose the easier path. I never had the motivation to put in my best effort at school. Although I completed all requirements satisfactorily, my performance in secondary school was not exceptional.

After secondary school, I initially chose to study ICT at ROC Mondriaan Tinwerf. My grades were generally quite decent, but my attendance and motivation in this program were also lacking.

In reality, I did not want to pursue further education and preferred to start working as a painter. However, my father insisted that I complete at least a minimum level of education before leaving school.

Since I became a painter, I chose an MBO Level 2 program. I successfully completed this program and even achieved a perfect score of 10/10 on the mathematics examination.

Working as a Painter

I then started working as a house painter, a profession I pursued for approximately ten years with great enjoyment, and which I still occasionally practice.

Nevertheless, software development continued to intrigue me. I attempted to teach myself the Python programming language, but I was quickly overwhelmed by the vast amount of information required to write even a simple program.

There was only one option left for me: returning to school. This time, I chose to enroll in the Software Developer program at mboRijnland in Leiden.

Switching to Software Developer

This time, I began the program with a tremendous amount of motivation, which paid off on multiple occasions. I was repeatedly asked to take on extra projects such as supervising an exchange program and was even given the opportunity to mentor HBO students as a challenge provider for their project.

The results of my motivation are also reflected in my grades. I graduated cum laude as the top student in the Software Development program at mboRijnland. I should note that seven years ago ROC Leiden transitioned into mboRijnland, but it is still an achievement I am extremely proud of!

Furthermore, I participated in an AI research project for which I received a certificate of excellence. This research focused on how schools can responsibly handle the use of AI. I conducted various studies for this project and delivered multiple presentations together with a team of students and a teacher.

School provided me with a solid foundation to build upon. By paying close attention and completing my assignments diligently, I am now able to write code independently and solve problems reasonably well.

Internship

Webprepare

I completed my first internship at Webprepare in Voorschoten, a company specializing in WordPress and hosting.

Battlebot

Battlebot was my final internship at mboRijnland within the Technology department. My task, together with a fellow student, was to fully automate the existing robot.

Future Toughts

Given my age and the other steps I wish to take in life, I am currently unable to continue formal studies, although I would have very much liked to do so.

Nevertheless, I am highly motivated to further expand my knowledge, building on the strong foundation that school has provided me.

For now, I am considering focusing on mastering a programming language thoroughly, while simultaneously continuing to study computer theory in general.

During the Battlebot internship, we worked with bit operators and hexadecimal values. I find this aspect of computing extremely interesting and would like to learn more about it.

In the near future, I plan to study a low-level programming language in order to gain practical experience in this area.
